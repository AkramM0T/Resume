Store Management System

This was a project I started at May 2021 to help my brother manage his business to record sales and generate monthly reports to elevate his business and give him more control.

The system provide the options to record each order with the number of products and prices with the customer name.

The user has the options to search, edit and add new client, provider and products.

User can track each client history.

User can also generate reports of the current month to compare between days, weeks or compare between two months.

This took me 4 weeks to complete and test it.
