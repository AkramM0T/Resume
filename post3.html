Environmental Assesment Project

This was a project I started at March 2021 to help RSK Company buiding and generating assesment reports to different enviromental outcomes.

The system provide the options to each between different receptors, no. of phases and other degree of effects.

The user has the options to store and save reports or to add more data or edit previous ones.
